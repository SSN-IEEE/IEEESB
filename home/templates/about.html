About SSN Student Branch

The IEEE student branch of SSN College of Engineering is very active and vibrant for organizing many events to promote technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. It’s objective is to create awareness regarding technological developments and to inspire, enable, empower and energize the student members to enhance their technical interests by providing them a platform to showcase their skills.



Vision

The primary purpose of IEEE student branch is to Inspire, Enable, Empower and Energize our student members to enhance their technical interests by providing them a platform to showcase their skills.



Mission


  • Engage the members with latest technologies
  • Work towards inspiring more students about IEEE
  • Kindle students interest in making new papers and projects
  • Organize technology awareness programs for young professionals
  • Form more Special Interest Groups to encourage project activity among the student members
  • Strive towards achieving more IEEE-sponsored awards and aim at representing papers in international conferences

Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS)

Your global connection to the world of biomedical engineering

IEEE Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society (EMBS) is the world’s largest international society of biomedical engineers.


IEEE ComSoc

IEEE Communication Society

IEEE ComSoc is a leading global community comprised of a diverse group of industry professionals with a common interest in advancing all communications technologies.


IEEE Women In Engineering

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests to a career in engineering.
